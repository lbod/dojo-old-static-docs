dijit._editor.plugins.AlwaysShowToolbar (version 1.3)

Object » dijit._editor._Plugin » dijit._editor.plugins.AlwaysShowToolbar

Usage:

var foo = new dijit._editor.plugins.AlwaysShowToolbar();

For auto-expanding editors, this plugin will keep the editor's toolbar visible even when the top of the editor has scrolled off the top of the viewport (usually when editing a long document).
