dojox.layout.RotatorContainer (version 1.3)

Object » dijit.layout.StackContainer » dojox.layout.RotatorContainer

Usage:

var foo = new dojox.layout.RotatorContainer();

Extends a StackContainer to automatically transition between children and display navigation in the form of tabs or a pager.
