dojox/grid/enhanced/plugins/IndirectSelection (version 1.8)

dojox/grid/enhanced/_Plugin,

A handy way for adding check boxe/radio button for rows, and selecting rows by swiping(or keyboard)

Usage:

var foo = new dojox/grid/enhanced/plugins/IndirectSelection();
Defined by dojox/grid/enhanced/plugins/IndirectSelection
