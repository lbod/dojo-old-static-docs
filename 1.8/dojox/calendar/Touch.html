dojox/calendar/Touch (version 1.8)

This plugin is managing the touch interactions on item renderers displayed by a calendar view.

See the dojox/calendar/Touch reference documentation for more information.

Property Summary

  • autoScrollTouchMargin The minimum number of minutes of margin around the edited event.
  • touchEndEditingTimer The delay after which the item is leaving the editing mode after the previous editing gesture, in touch context.
  • touchStartEditingTimer The delay of one touch over the renderer before setting the item in editing mode.

Method Summary

  • _docEditingTouchEndHandler(e)
  • _docEditingTouchMoveHandler(e)
  • _getTouchesOnRenderers(e, item) Returns the touch indices that are on a editing handles or body of the renderers
  • _startTouchItemEditingGesture(e) Determines if a editing gesture is starting according to touches.
  • postMixInProperties()

Event Summary

  • _onRendererTouchStart(e, renderer)
